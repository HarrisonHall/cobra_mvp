COBRA App

Our COBRA App is an app backed by the Comprehensive Oculomotor Behavioral Response Assessment technology that can screen for neurological disorders or injury detecting oculomotor signatures which allows medical staff to localize the affected brain region and characterize the nature of a mild deficit in a timely manner.

The cobra app shown below can be downloaded on any mobile device.
